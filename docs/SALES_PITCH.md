## 🎯 Key Features

### 📊 Dashboard & Analytics
- **Real-time Financial Overview** - At-a-glance KPIs (total invoiced, collected, expenses, net position)
- **Outstanding Receivables Tracking** - Monitor unpaid invoices by customer
- **Payment Collection Rates** - Track cash inflows vs. invoiced amounts
- **Monthly Trend Analysis** - 6-month rolling charts of invoices, collections, and expenses
- **Top Customers Report** - Identify your highest-value and riskiest accounts
- **Expense Dashboard** - Recent expenses by category with spending trends

### 👥 Customer Management
- **Complete Customer Database** - Store names, contact info, company details
- **Contact History** - Track all interactions and document references
- **Outstanding Balance Tracking** - Real-time aging of customer receivables
- **Customer Statements** - Generate and email detailed statements with transaction history & aging
- **Quick Search & Filters** - Find customers instantly

### 📄 Quotation Management
- **Professional Quotation Generation** - Branded, item-level quotes with taxes & discounts
- **Status Tracking** - Draft, Sent, Accepted, Rejected workflow
- **Validity Period Management** - Set expiration dates for quotes
- **One-click Convert to Invoice** - Turn accepted quotes into invoices instantly

### 🧾 Invoice Management
- **Auto-numbered Invoices** - Unique invoice reference for compliance
- **Multiple Line Items** - Flexible line item descriptions and amounts
- **Tax & Discount Calculations** - Automatic or manual tax/discount application
- **Payment Status Tracking** - Draft, Sent, Partial, Overdue, Paid statuses
- **Partial Payment Support** - Record part payments and track remaining balance
- **Professional PDF Generation** - Beautifully formatted, downloadable invoices

### 💰 Smart Payment & Receipt Management
- **Multi-Invoice Allocation** - Allocate a single receipt/payment across multiple customer invoices
- **Multiple Payment Methods** - Cash, Card, Bank Transfer, Check, Online Payment
- **Receipt Generation** - Auto-numbered receipt records with payment details
- **Payment Allocation Tracking** - See exactly which receipt applies to which invoice
- **Payment History** - Complete audit trail per customer and per invoice
- **Receipt PDF Export** - Generate receipt documents for records

### 💼 Expense Management
- **Expense Tracking** - Record operational expenses with category, vendor, and payment method
- **Expense Categories** - Pre-built and custom categories (Operations, Office Supplies, Utilities, etc.)
- **Payment Method Recording** - Track how expenses were paid
- **Vendor Management** - Log vendor names and reference numbers
- **Expense Notes** - Attach descriptions and notes to each expense

### 📈 Financial Reporting
- **Customer Statement Generation** - Detailed ledger view showing all invoices, receipts, and running balance for each customer
- **Statement Aging** - Automatic aging buckets (Current, 1-30 days, 31-60 days, 61-90 days, 90+ days overdue)
- **Monthly Statement Runs** - Batch generate statements for all customers for a given period
- **Statement Status Tracking** - Mark statements as generated or emailed with timestamp and recipient tracking
- **Profit & Loss (P&L) Reporting** - Comprehensive profitability analysis:
  - **Invoiced Revenue** - Total revenue recognized in the period
  - **Collected Revenue** - Cash actually received
  - **Total Expenses** - All operational expenses
  - **Net Profit (Cash Basis)** - Collected revenue minus expenses
  - **Net Profit (Accrual Basis)** - Invoiced revenue minus expenses
  - **Expense Breakdown by Category** - Deep dive into where money is spent
  - **Date Range Filtering** - Analyze any custom period
- **Accounts Receivable Aging** - See customer balances broken down by days overdue
- **Collection Rate Analysis** - Track cash collection vs. invoiced amounts
- **Monthly Trend Analysis** - Historical P&L comparison across months
